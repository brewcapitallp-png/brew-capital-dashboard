THE 5-GATE SYSTEM EXPLAINED
Gate 1 — Zacks Signal
Zacks Rank 1 or 2 required. ESP (Earnings Surprise Prediction) above 3%. VGM grade A or B. This is your starting filter — only the top stocks pass. Think of it as the bouncer at the door.
Gate 2 — LevelFields Catalyst
An approved catalyst event with win rate above 60%. Approved events: buybacks, insider buying, FDA approvals, earnings beats, M&A. This is the reason the stock moves — you need a specific trigger.
Gate 3 — Technical Setup
Stock must be above 50MA and 200MA. Volume above 150% of average. RSI between 45-65. RSI(2) below 20 for optimal entry timing. The chart must confirm the fundamental story.
Gate 4 — Kelly Risk/Reward
Risk/reward ratio must exceed 2.5:1. Portfolio correlation below 0.65. Fractional Kelly sizing at 25-50% of theoretical maximum. IF THIS GATE FAILS = SKIP. No exceptions. This protects your capital.
Gate 5 — Regime Check
VIX level determines sizing. SPY must be above 200MA. No FOMC meetings or earnings within 14 days of entry. The macro environment must support the trade.
